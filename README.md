# Dusk Support

  **Dusk** is a sound mixer for sleep, focus, and deep work.

  ## Contact

  Email: **contact@verin.tech**

  We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

  ## Common Questions

  **How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
  Open Dusk → Settings → Restore Purchase.

  **The app won't play audio.**
  Check your phone isn't in silent mode. If your device uses the ring/silent switch, Dusk still respects it for ambient audio.

  **Can I get a refund?**
  Refunds are handled by Apple. Please use reportaproblem.apple.com.
